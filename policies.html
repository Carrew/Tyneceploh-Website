School Policies

Admission Policy

Anyone wishing to be admitted into Tyneceploh Educational Foundation must meet the following requirements:

  1. Successfully pass entrance exam and interview.
  2. Submit a filled in purchased admission form.
  3. Attach two (2) passport size photos to admission form.
  4. Provide recent medical certificate.
  5. Letters of Recommendation, one (1) from past school and one (1) from pastor or community leader.
  6. Submit an official transcript from past school.
  7. Submit all requirements in one Manilla folder.

Registration Policy

The submission of all the requirements plus payment of fees according to payment plan completes the registration.

Academic Policies

While we endeavor to seek academic excellence, we subscribe to the Ministry of Education minimum passing grade of 70%. However, we encourage students to strive for higher marks by regularly recognizing and rewarding gifts or promotion. Students who maintain 95% average from first period through the first semester will be promoted to the next class except where the student is in the highest class of the school. A student failing in one subject in the average for the year will be promoted under condition and is compelled to attend enrichment classes but will still have to be monitored during the first period in the new class. A student failing in two (2) or more subjects repeats the class.
