About Us

Tyneceploh Educational Foundation is a Christ-centered academic institution serving nursery to 9th grade students with quality education in Rehab Road, ELWA, Liberia.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of academic excellence and Christian character, developing students into godly leaders who will influence the world positively.

Our Mission

To provide a sound Christian and academic foundation to every student through dedicated teaching, discipline, and prayer.

History

Founded in 2018, Tyneceploh Educational Foundation began with a vision to offer both academic and spiritual training to young minds. Since then, we have grown in excellence, community impact, and student population.

Core Values

  • PASSION: A commitment to excellence with care and enthusiasm.
  • EXCELLENCE: Pursuing quality in all we do.
  • RESPECT: Treating others with love, integrity, and openness.
  • TRUTH: Living and learning by God's truth and applying it in life.
