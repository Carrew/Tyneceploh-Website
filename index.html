About Us

Tyneceploh Educational Foundation is a nursery to 9th-grade school located at Rehab Road, ELWA, Liberia. With a vision to empower every child with holistic education, the school has served the community since 2018 and currently has over 100 students and 10 dedicated teachers.

Mission

To nurture young minds through quality education, moral values, and community engagement, preparing them for lifelong success.

Vision

To be a leading institution in Liberia providing inclusive, affordable, and high-standard education from nursery to junior high level.

Our History

Founded in 2018, Tyneceploh Educational Foundation began as a small nursery and has grown to include classes up to 9th grade, earning the trust of parents and producing excellent academic results.
